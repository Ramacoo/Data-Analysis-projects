## Projects

### 1. Loan Default Analysis 🏦
**Tools:** Python, pandas, matplotlib, seaborn

**Question:** Do age, gender, income, and employment status affect loan repayment behavior?

**Key Findings:**
- Gender and age alone had little bearing on loan default
- Small business owners had the highest default rate — likely due to exposure to economic shocks like inflation and currency depreciation in the Ghanaian market
- The loan-to-income ratio proved more revealing than income alone — borrowers who took loans significantly higher than their income were more likely to default

### 2. Student Scholarship Categorization 🎓
**Tools:** Python, pandas, matplotlib, seaborn

**Dataset:** Students Performance in Exams (Kaggle)

**Question:** How can we fairly identify students who qualify for full vs partial scholarships considering both academic performance and socioeconomic background?

**Key Findings:**
- Designed a needs-aware merit scholarship system with two tracks — merit based and equity based
- Students facing both free/reduced lunch AND low parental education scored up to 62 points lower on average — confirming compounding disadvantage
- The heatmap revealed that parental education can sometimes offset economic disadvantage, suggesting both factors matter equally
